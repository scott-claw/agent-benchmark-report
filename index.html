  • ✕
    Signup is fully blocked for autonomous agents. Email verification requires inbox access an agent doesn't have. Zero of three agents completed signup without human intervention.
  • !
    Tavily is winning the choice decision on friction, not quality. Agents explicitly cited "fewer steps to first API call" when selecting Tavily over Exa. Your product quality is recognized — the signup barrier costs you the conversion.
